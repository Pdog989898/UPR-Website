How to Install Your UPR Streaming Box

Step-by-step instructions with images and tips for setup.

  1. Unbox the UPR Streaming Box and accessories.
  2. Connect to your TV using HDMI.
  3. Plug in the power adapter and turn it on.
  4. Follow the on-screen setup instructions.
  5. Connect to Wi-Fi or wired internet.
  6. Enjoy streaming!
